About SunnySide Bakery

Bringing warmth, comfort, and freshly baked joy to our community.

Our Story

SunnySide Bakery started as a small family kitchen in 2015, when founder Maria Lopez began baking sourdough loaves for neighbors and friends. What began as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a beloved local bakery.

Today, we continue to bake in small batches using locally sourced flour, free-range eggs, and real butter. Every loaf, cake, and pastry is made by hand with simple ingredients and a lot of heart.
